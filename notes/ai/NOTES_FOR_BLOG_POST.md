> The log operates on the receiver. The contributor's log is affected by how many other cards value *them*. So the metric doesn't directly enforce the "budget" property on the contribution side. But it does enforce it on the receiving side, which achieves something similar: every card needs to be wanted, and being *very* wanted has diminishing returns.

— On why incoming log-synergy naturally prevents monoculture without explicitly modeling archetype budgets.
